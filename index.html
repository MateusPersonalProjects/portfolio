About Me

Hello, my name is Mateus, I'm 24 years old and I'm a Computer Science student at the Federal University of Paran√° (UFPR)! My first contact with development was at the age of 14 when I joined a technical school, where I was introduced to various programming languages such as C++, Java, PHP, JavaScript, and was able to work on various web development projects.
